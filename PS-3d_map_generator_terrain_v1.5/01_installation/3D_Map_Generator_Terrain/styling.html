Help

1. Change and design the terrain surface with the gradient presets or texturize with the seamless textures and brushes

2. If you edit the gradients or textures do not forget to press „save“ in the panel

3. Change and style the background with the presets behind the ground button

4. Add contour lines with your own colors

5. Raise or lower the water level

6. Change the map layer height

7. Set the global light

8. You can also draw trees and water onto the surface. Therefore you have to click on the corresponding symbol in the surface drawing tools

TIPP: minimize Photoshop during the generation process to shorten the editing time significantly
